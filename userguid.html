User Guide

Getting Started

Here are the steps to get started with our exam paper management system:

  1. Select the role you belogs to from navigation links.
  2. Click it , and enter the login credentials.
  3. Use login credentials provided to you.

Exam-Setter

Here are some tips for Exam-Setter:

  • You can login with your login credentials.
  • You will see the question papers submitted by you.
  • If you didnt submitted any question paper, you can submit it by clicking on Upload a question paper button.
  • You can ddelete or reupload a question paper.
  • Once you have submitted a question paper Paper-Checker can review it.
  • Paper-Checker can approve or disapprove your question paper, if it is disapproved, Paper-Checker can give you a reason for that, Based on that reason you can resubmit question paper.
  • If Paper-Checker approved your question paper it will be sent to examination cell for printing.
  • You can see the status of your question paper in your page.
  • You cant delete or reupload a question paper if they are approved by Paper-Checker.
  • You can delete or reupload a question paper if they are disapproved by Paper-Checker or until the Paper-Checker didnt take any action, means till the status is defaut(-).

Paper-Checker

Here are some tips for Paper-Checker:

  • You can login with your login credentials.
  • You will see the question papers submitted by Exam-Setter in your page.
  • You can approve or disapprove the question paper submitted by you department Exam-Setters.
  • You have to give a reason if you disapproving question paper.
  • Once you disapproved a question paper, Exam-Setter can resubmit the que paper and you can check it again.
  • If you approved a question paper, and now you want to disapprove it you can do that also and the question paper is being removed from examination cell also.
  • If you disapprove a question paper and later you want to approve it before Exam-Setter can reupload or delete it you can do that also, and the reason you provided will be changed to (-).
  • You can change the name for Paper-Checker.
  • You can register a new Exam-Setter(Login Credentials).
  • You can update a Exam-Setter data based on either course name or course code or both.
  • You can see the all Exam-Setter details in Delete Exam-Setter Page, and you can delete a particular Exam-Setter login credentials through that page.
  • Once you deleted the Exam-Setter detials, that Exam-Setter would no longer be able to login to the system.

Examination Cell

Here are some tips for Paper-Checker:

  • You can login with your login credentials.
  • You will see the all question papers approved by Paper-Checker's.
  • You can see Exam-Setter name , branch , course name, course code and other necessary information.
  • You can directly print the question papers by clicking on print button appearing next to each aproved question paper record.
  • If you approved a question paper, and now you want to disapprove it you can do that also and the question paper is being removed from examination cell also.
